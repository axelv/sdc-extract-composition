Recommendation
Option 5 (Minimal Inline) integrates best with the new formula bar - keeps everything together, shows preview.

Option 3 (Toggle List) is best for non-technical users - familiar toggle UI, pre-defined options, no confusing terminology.

Option 1 (Compact Chips) is a good middle ground - less space than current, supports reordering via drag.
